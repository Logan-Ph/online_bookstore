The Shadow of the Wind by Carlos Ruiz Zafón - This captivating novel is set in Barcelona in the aftermath of the Spanish Civil War.

It follows the story of Daniel Sempere, a young boy who discovers a mysterious book called The Shadow of the Wind by an unknown author named Julian Carax. As he delves deeper into Carax's past, he uncovers a web of secrets and lies that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear.

With its vivid descriptions of Barcelona and its intricate plot, The Shadow of the Wind is a literary masterpiece that will keep you hooked until the very end.
